# Public Launch Guide

DataCenterLedger Explorer is a public-data review workbench for organizing data center records as source-backed claims.

It is designed for civic transparency, research triage, and careful claim review. It is not designed for private discovery, targeting, or security-sensitive enrichment.

## Launch posture

Use the live app to show the workflow, not to publish a complete national registry.

Recommended launch language:

> DataCenterLedger Explorer helps reviewers import public records, preserve source receipts, score confidence, track lifecycle decisions, and export reviewable Ledger packets.

## What to show publicly

- The live app
- Demo rows
- CSV templates
- public-data boundary docs
- screenshots of the review workflow
- exported demo Ledger packets

## What not to publish by default

- private notes
- unreviewed exact coordinates
- facility access details
- scraped records with unclear terms
- security layouts or operational details
- a consolidated dataset that has not passed public-boundary review

## Safe demo flow

1. Open the live app.
2. Point out the demo-data warning.
3. Explain the public-data boundary pills.
4. Click a demo record.
5. Review receipts and blockers.
6. Export a launch packet.
7. Explain that raw imports are claims until reviewed.
